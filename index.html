Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for medical travel?

Yes, you’ll require a medical visa. We provide full visa assistance including an invitation letter from the hospital.

What is included in the package?

Our package covers doctor consultation, hospital stay, airport transfers, and language interpreter (if needed).

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. Your medical records are encrypted and shared only with your consent for medical review.
